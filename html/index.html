Exterior Inspection:

Check for any visible damage or scratches on the body. Inspect the condition of headlights, taillights, and turn signals. Check windshield and rearview mirrors for cracks or damage. Ensure all tires are properly inflated and in good condition. Test windshield wipers and washer fluid functionality.

Interior Inspection:

Check the functionality of all seatbelts. Inspect the condition of seats, including any tears or damage. Test all interior lights and dashboard indicators. Check the functionality of the horn and steering wheel.

Engine Inspection:

Check the engine oil level and condition. Inspect the coolant level and ensure there are no leaks. Test the battery for proper voltage and connections. Check the air filter and replace if necessary. Inspect the condition of drive belts and hoses.

Undercarriage Inspection:

Inspect the condition of the exhaust system. Check for any leaks in the fuel or brake lines. Inspect the suspension system for any visible damage. Check the condition of brake pads and rotors. Ensure the proper functioning of the parking brake.

Safety and Miscellaneous:

Check the functionality of all doors, including locks and windows. Test the operation of the air conditioning and heating systems. Inspect the windshield for any cracks or chips. Check the functionality of the audio system, including speakers. Ensure that all necessary documents, such as registration and insurance, are present.
